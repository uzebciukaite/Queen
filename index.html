About QUEEN

British rock band Queen blended heavy metal, glam rock, and camp theatrics to make it one of the most popular groups of the 1970s. Queen was formed in London in 1971 when the members of two bands composed of university and art-school students joined together.

Members

Group Queen consists of 4 members:

  • Freddie Mercury
  • Brian May
  • Roger Taylor
  • John Deacon

Legacy

Queen have more recently been recognized as having made significant contributions to such genres as arena rock, glam rock, hard rock, heavy metal, pop rock, and progressive rock, amongst others.

Though it's been decades since the original lineup of Queen were writing and performing together, the band remain the most important in music history thanks to their creation of dramatic, anthemic and inspiring songs which never get old no matter how many times you've heard them.
